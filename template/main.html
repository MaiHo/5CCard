What are we?

Have you ever walked back to your dorm at 11:50 pm on a Saturday night and then all of a sudden...! You wondered whether or not you flexed out yet?! We all know the CUC website is pretty slow, bad, and ugly. So our web application sidesteps that nasty experience for you! As long as you have a current CUC account, you can log into our app and then you'll see your flex balance, claremont cash balance, and how many meals you have left. This app was made for the 2013 5C Hackathon.
